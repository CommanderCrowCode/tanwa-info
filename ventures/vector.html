Overview

Vector is building the future of hybrid fitness training. Focused on HYROX and functional fitness, we use AI to create personalized training programs that optimize both running endurance and functional workout performance.

HYROX combines 8km of running with 8 functional workout stations. Training for this unique format requires balancing multiple energy systems, strength modalities, and movement patterns. Vector's AI understands these complex interactions.
